About

The dynamic and cutting-edge business Proximus Media specializes in social media management. Our goal is to assist our clients in utilizing the power of social media to connect people and companies. Effective social media tactics that promote development, engagement, and brand exposure are the focus of our team of specialists. Regardless of how big or small your company is, we can help you improve your online presence and reach your social media goals. With Proximus Media, let us bring your brand closer to your audience.

  • Continuously adjusts strategies based on performance analytics to achieve desired results

Services

At Proximus Media, we offer a comprehensive range of social media management services designed to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Social media management

This includes creating and scheduling posts, managing and responding to comments and messages, and monitoring social media analytics to track engagement and performance

Content creation

Developing and producing content, such as videos, photos, and written posts, for use on social media platforms

Community management

Building and managing an online community of customers or fans on social media platforms

Social media advertising

Creating and managing social media advertising campaigns to reach specific audiences and goals

Reputation management

Monitoring and addressing negative comments or reviews on social media platforms

Analytics and reporting

Providing data and insights on social media performance and audience demographics

Event videography

Live-streaming events including conferences, trade exhibitions, concerts, and more for clients to use for marketing or archival purposes

Film and video post-production

Creating the finished result requires editing and polishing raw video and film production footage.

Drone videography

Capturing aerial footage using drones to provide unique perspectives and breathtaking views for clients.
